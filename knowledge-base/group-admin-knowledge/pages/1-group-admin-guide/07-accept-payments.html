AllPlayers.com allows groups to apply to accept payments online through AllPlayers.com.

Some common uses of this feature are:

  • Accept payment at the time of registration for sports leagues and teams.
  • Allow members to make a donation to your organization.
  • Create groups for events and tournaments, and accept payments when members register for the event.
  • Allow members to purchase products such as apparel and memorabilia.

If you already have a group setup to accept payments...

If you already have a group setup to accept payments and would like to setup another, please contact support.

If you do not have any groups setup to accept payments...

If you do not have any groups setup to accept payments, please visit 26fin.com to obtain a MID.

In most cases, it will take around 2 days to setup your group to receive payments.

Configure store settings

Once your group is setup to receive payments, you can setup your group store to allow you to create products and charge registration fees.

To setup your store:

Navigate to the group who's store you would like to setup.

In the group toolbox, navigate to Store.
