As a group admin, you can upload electronic files to your group pages that your members can download. Your files might be forms that your members should complete, a PDF of your league rulebook, end-of-season parent evaluations, or whatever you may need to distribute to your team members.

Downloads is a group feature that you can enable or disable based on your needs. Before beginning, ensure that Downloads is enabled by visiting Administrative Tools > Features in your Group Toolbox.

Add downloads box to dashboard

Click Design Page to show your Dashboard.


Choose Downloads from the Add dropdown menu


Drag the File Downloads box onto your page.


See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your Dashboard.


When you have placed the File Downloads box on your page, click Save Changes in your Dashboard.

Upload your file

In your Group Toolbox, navigate to Upload > Upload Files.


Fill out the Title field, choose a file to upload, click the Upload button, select the groups you would like to share the file with, and click Save.


Click your group name to return to your group's dashboard.


Your new file will now appear in your FILES box on your group's dashboard. You can edit or remove your file at any time by clicking the Edit link, which only group administrators will see.
