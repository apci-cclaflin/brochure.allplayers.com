As a group administrator, you have access to buttons and links in your Group Toolbox that other group members will not see.

The buttons and links that appear in your Group Toolbox are based on the features you have enabled for the group.


The most common tools used by group administrators appear as buttons, so you have quick access to communicate and share with your group members.


More group management tools appear when you hover over these buttons.


You can close the Group Toolbox by clicking anywhere outside of it.
