As a group admin, you have the ability to cancel an order made by one of your group members.

To cancel an order made by one of your group members:

Navigate to your group.

Click the Toolbox button.


To cancel an order made by one of your group members:

Navigate to your group.

In the group toolbox, navigate to Store.

This will bring you to your group's orders report.


You can use the filter on the left of the page to more easily find the order you wish to cancel.


If you are having trouble finding the order you wish to cancel, make sure you have the correct order states selected in the filter. You can learn more about Order States here.


Once you have found the order that you wish to cancel, click the checkbox next to it and click Cancel Order(s).


Review the orders you have selected.

Once you are sure that you have selected the correct orders, click Confirm.
