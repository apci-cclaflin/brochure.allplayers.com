As a group administrator, you can easily add, move, or remove members in your groups using your Manage Members tools.

To add, move or remove members in groups:

Navigate to your group.

Click the Toolbox button.


In the group toolbox, navigate to Manage > Members.


By default, all of the members of your current group and its subgroups will display.

Filtering results

There are several filters available to help you find only the members you want.

Groups

Allows you to choose which groups you would like the members list to be populated from. You can choose between your current group and any of its subgroups.

Member search

Allows you to search for a specific group member by name.

Select form

If you have a webform for your group, this allows you to change the displayed fields to those captured by the webform.

Select Fields to Display

Allows you to select which fields you would like to display.

Role

Allows you to limit your results to those with specific roles.

View items per page

Allows you to limit how many items will appear on a single page.

Select members

Click the checkbox next to member(s) that you want to change.

Add member(s) to group(s)

This option will allow you to add a member who is already in your current group hierarchy to another group in your current group hierarchy.

After selecting the members you wish to perform the operation on...

In the - Choose an operation - dropdown, select Add member(s) to group(s), and click Execute.


Select the group(s) that you would like the member(s) added to, then click Next.


Select the role(s) that you would like to assign to the member(s).

Select whether or not to send out a notification to the users involved in this operation.

When you are ready to process the operation, click Confirm

Move member(s) to group(s)

This option will allow you to move a member who is already in your current group hierarchy, from one group, to another group in your current group hierarchy.

After selecting the members you wish to perform the operation on...

In the - Choose an operation - dropdown, select Move member(s) to group(s), and click Execute.


Select the group(s) that you would like the member(s) moved from and added to, then click Next.


Select the role(s) that you would like to assign to the member(s).

Select whether or not to send out a notification to the users involved in this operation.

When you are ready to process the operation, click Confirm

Remove member(s) from group(s)

This option will allow you to remove a member who is already in your current group hierarchy from one or multiple groups.

After selecting the members you wish to perform the operation on...

In the - Choose an operation - dropdown, select Remove member(s) from group(s), and click Execute.


Select the group(s) that you would like the member(s) removed from, then click Next.


Select whether or not to send out a notification to the users involved in this operation.

When you are ready to process the operation, click Confirm
