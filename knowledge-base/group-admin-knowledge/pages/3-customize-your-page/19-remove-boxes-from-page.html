Just as you can add boxes to your page at any time, you can remove them easily.

To remove your boxes:

Navigate to your group.

Click Design Page to show your Dashboard.


With your Dashboard open, move your cursor over boxes on your page. As your cursor goes over a box, controls will appear in the upper right corner of the boxes.


To remove a box from your page, click the X icon.


Once you've removed the boxes that you do not want to show on your page, click Save Changes in your Dashboard. Your Dashboard will close and you'll see your new layout.


When you remove a box from your page, you're simply hiding it. You're not deleting the box forever. The box you remove disappears from your page, but it's always saved in your Dashboard. You can place the box back on your page at any time through your Dashboard.
