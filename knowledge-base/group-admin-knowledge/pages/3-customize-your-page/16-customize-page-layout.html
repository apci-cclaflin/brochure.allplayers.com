The Dashboard allows you to change the layout of your group page. It also allows you to customize your page with additional boxes to provide more information to your group.

To change the layout of your group's page:

Navigate to your group.

Click Design Page to show your Dashboard.


There are multiple layouts that you can choose for your group's page

  • Default sets your page layout to three columns, with a sidebar on the left and two columns of content area filling the remainder of the page.
  • Two Columns sets your page layout to two equally sized columns with a sidebar on the left and a content area filling the remainder of the page.
  • Left Sidebar sets your page layout to two columns, with a thin sidebar on the left and a content area filling the remainder of the page.
  • Right Sidebar sets your page layout to two columns, with a thin sidebar on the right and a content area filling the remainder of the page.

You can select a new layout using the Layout dropdown.


Once you've changed your layout option, click Save. Your Dashboard will close and you'll see your new layout.
