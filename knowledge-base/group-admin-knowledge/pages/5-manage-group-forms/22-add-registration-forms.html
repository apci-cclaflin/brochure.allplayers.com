As a group administrator, you can add registration forms for any or all of the roles associated with your group. These forms will be presented to your members during the group registration process. You can create unique registration forms for each role if you need to collect different information. For example, your registration form for coaches might ask for different information than your registration form for players.

Make it easy for members to register for your group. Only ask your participants for the information you truly require during the registration process. Adding extra optional fields will make it less likely that your users will complete your group registration quickly and easily.

To add registration forms to your group:

Navigate to your group.

Click the Toolbox button.


In the group toolbox, navigate to Registration > Registration.


Select the Individual Registration tab.


You can assign one or more forms to each role in your group. If your group has existing forms, you have the option to use them as a registration form.

Scroll to the role to which you would like to assign a registration form and click its name to expand it.


Click Edit in the Registration Forms box.

Then click Add new registration form to be taken to the Create a form tool.

See Create a form for instructions on how to create your new registration form.


If your group already has one or more registration forms that you would like to use, select them from the box.


Once you have finished adding forms to any roles that you wish, click Save Configuration and you're done! Registration for any of the roles that you selected will now include the form you chose.
