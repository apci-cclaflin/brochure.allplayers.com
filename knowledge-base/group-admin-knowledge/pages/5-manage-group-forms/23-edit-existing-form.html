As a group administrator, you can edit and update forms associated with your group.

To edit a form:

Navigate to your group.

Click the Toolbox button.


In the group toolbox, navigate to Forms > Manage Forms.


On your Manage your group forms page, you can view all of the forms associated with your group.

To update a form, click the name of the form that you would like to update.


Click Edit at the top of the page.


Edit any of the information on your form.


Once you have completed all of the updates, click Save and you're finished.

Your form should now reflect all of the changes that you made for any future form submissions.
