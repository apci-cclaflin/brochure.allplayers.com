Websites that are collecting information from children under the age of thirteen are required to comply with Federal Trade Commission Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). COPPA is just the starting point for security at AllPlayers.com. We raise the compliance bar with our COPPA+ Guardian Watch. COPPA+ Guardian Watch is the new standard in protecting children online, and we are proud to say we did it first. COPPA+ Guardian Watch is the first service that verifies a child's guardian through face-to-face confirmation by a trusted source, like a coach, teacher or mentor. It keeps the parent in total control 100% of the time. Parents and guardians control what features are accessible to their children. This includes viewing friends and messages. Parents can always stay informed, aware and on guard. AllPlayers.com takes the privacy of our members and their families seriously.

How do I add specific information to my group page?

You can add specific information to your group page by placing a custom box on your group's dashboard. You can learn more about putting custom boxes on your dashboard here.

×

How can I customize a webform?

You can create a webform with a variety of fields to collect your member’s information. If you need to collect additional information and require a custom field, please contact our Member Services Department.

×

How can I change my group calendar’s time zone?

You can change your group calendar’s time zone by navigating to Group Toolbox > Administrative Tools > Features and clicking Settings under Calendar. Your group’s members will still be able to view the calendar in their selected time zone. You can learn more about your group calendar here.

×

How do groups work?

Groups on AllPlayers.com are designed to feature an organization's activities for their members. A group can be anything from a camp, to a tryout, to a team page. For more information on building and customizing your group, please check out all of our Group Admin Knowledge.

×

How can I open my group for registration?

You can open your group for registration by navigating to Group Toolbox > Administrative Tools > Registration and configuring your registration settings. You can learn more about opening your group for registration here.

×

Are you integrated with other software (quickbooks/quckscores)?

AllPlayers.com is proud to be based on an open-source platform which gives us the scalability to integrate with several productivity applications including QuickScores.com. To find out more, contact AllPlayers.com.

×

Is posting an announcement the same as sending a broadcast message?

Posting an announcement is different from sending a broadcast message. An announcement will show up on your group’s dashboard as well as your group’s members personal homepages. A broadcast message will be sent to your selected group’s members AllPlayers message inbox and possibly by email or sms depending on their chosen notification settings. You can learn more about sending broadcast messages here.

How do I find a group?

You can easily find a group using the Find a group tool. Just click Find a group at the top of any page to start searching! You can learn more about finding groups here.

×

How do I make a payment (locate invoices/orders)?

You can easily make a payment on an invoice/order by navigating to Orders > Outstanding Invoices in your navigation bar. Then select the invoice/order that you would like to pay and click Pay Selected.

×

How do I add family members?

You can add family members by clicking the Add family button on your home page and selecting either Add Child or Add Spouse/Co-Guardian. You can learn more about adding a child here and more about adding a spouse/co-guardian here.

×

How do I register myself/my child for a group?

You can register yourself and your child to a group by clicking on the Register or Join button on the group’s page. You can learn more about finding a group here and more about registering your child for a group here.

×

How does the family work on AllPlayers (Why should I connect my family on AllPlayers)?

When a parent creates an account on AllPlayers.com, they can connect all of their children under this one account. This allows them to register and manage their children’s information using a single login. Each child is then provided with their own account and can use the login information to view the information their team or organization posts through AllPlayers.com. For more information on connecting your family click here.

×

Is your site secure?

AllPlayers.com takes our users privacy seriously. We use the Extended Validation SSL certificate for HTTPS when transmitting information. This is strongest type of HTTPS that exists. To learn more about protecting your information, contact AllPlayers.com.

×

Why do I need to verify my account (can I still register with an unverified account)?

In order to gain access to many of the features on our site, you must verify your account. However, you do not need to be verified in order to register with an organization. You can learn more about creating and registering your account here.
